The indie queen herself, Lana Del Rey. An artist lost in the generation of the Techno. My ultimate. Lana Del Rey mainly writes and sings music about love and life: of beauty and destruction. She incorporates contrasting themes paired with mellow vibe to create wonderful music.

I am definitely in love with all of her songs. But here are my All-Time Top 3 lana Del Rey bops: How to disappear, Religion, and Not All Who Wander Are Lost. Prepare to get emotional.

Lorde


My first love: Lorde. She is probably known for her famous song Royals, but there is more to her than that. She only released 3 albums since her career started.
